Royal Saints Academy

School-Based Ballet & Dance Education Program

Introduction

Royal Saints Academy partners with nursery, primary, and secondary schools across Nigeria to deliver structured ballet and dance lessons directly within the school environment. Our program supports pupils’ physical development, discipline, confidence, and creativity — without the need for a dance studio.

Program Objectives

  • Improve posture, coordination, balance, and physical fitness
  • Build confidence, discipline, and teamwork among pupils
  • Encourage creativity and positive self-expression
  • Provide a quality extracurricular or curricular activity aligned with school values

Programs Offered

  • Early Years (Ages 3–5): Creative movement, rhythm, coordination, fun-based learning
  • Primary School (Ages 6–10): Ballet foundations, posture, musicality, flexibility
  • Upper Primary & Secondary: Ballet basics, contemporary dance, performance skills

How the Partnership Works

  • School contacts Royal Saints Academy
  • Assessment of available space and timetable
  • Weekly dance classes commence on agreed days
  • Optional end-of-term performance or school showcase

Why Nigerian Schools Choose Us

  • No special infrastructure required — we adapt to halls or classrooms
  • Trained instructors experienced with Nigerian children
  • Flexible scheduling during school hours or after school
  • Supports pupils’ confidence, posture, and overall development
