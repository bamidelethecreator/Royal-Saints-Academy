School-Based Ballet & Dance Program Proposal

A structured extracurricular dance initiative designed for Nigerian nursery, primary, and secondary schools.

About Royal Saints Academy

Royal Saints Academy is a professional dance institution committed to delivering high-quality ballet and creative dance education directly within school environments. Our programs are tailored to support physical development, confidence building, discipline, and creative expression among pupils and students.

Program Overview

Our school-based dance program is delivered during agreed curriculum or extracurricular periods and is structured according to age group and skill level.

  • Creative movement and ballet foundations for Nursery & Primary
  • Technique, posture, rhythm, and expression for Secondary schools
  • End-of-term performances and showcases (optional)
  • Professional instructors provided by Royal Saints Academy

Benefits to the School

  • Enhances pupils’ physical coordination and confidence
  • Adds value to the school’s extracurricular offerings
  • Supports holistic child development
  • Improves student engagement and school culture
  • No recruitment or training burden on school staff

Program Implementation

  • Classes held once or twice weekly (as agreed)
  • Each session lasts 45–60 minutes
  • Programs run during term time
  • Space required: hall, classroom, or open activity area

School Requirements

  • Designated class space during agreed time slots
  • Support with student coordination and communication
  • Approval for program inclusion as extracurricular activity
